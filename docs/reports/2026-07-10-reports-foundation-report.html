A durable money and inventory foundation for Athena reports

This delivery gives Athena a store-scoped, auditable reporting pipeline for sales, cost, inventory movement, settlements, service work, and Daily Close. It creates the backend foundation for the future Reports workspace; it does not ship that workspace UI or activate reporting in production.

Executive Summary

Money stays honest

Revenue, settlement, known COGS, uncovered cost, and valuation currency remain distinct. Profit is withheld when the evidence is incomplete or currencies do not align.

Inventory stays traceable

One governed inventory effect boundary records sales, returns, exchanges, services, expenses, receipts, adjustments, and corrections without mutating operational inventory during rebuilds.

Operations keep moving

Missing SKU unit cost never blocks a sale. Athena highlights incomplete coverage, routes a full admin to the existing product correction surface, and rebuilds affected projections.

Why This Foundation Was Needed

The intended Reports workspace must replace manual ledgers as the operator's source for money and items moving through the business. Existing operational records were useful, but they did not share a durable reporting identity, common currency semantics, consistent operating-day boundaries, or a replayable projection model.

The key product decision was to preserve trade continuity. Optional SKU unit cost remains optional at the operational boundary. Reporting records the known portion, exposes the uncovered quantity prominently, and routes correction work without imposing a cutoff or grace period.

Scope boundary: this branch delivers reporting contracts, facts, projections, health, maintenance, access control, source integration, and existing correction inputs. A new operator-facing Reports workspace is intentionally a later delivery.

The Mental Model

Treat reporting as two coupled consistency lanes created by the same source transition. Operational records remain authoritative for doing business. Reporting retains durable evidence and builds replaceable views, but public reads only use a store-specific generation after explicit activation.

Lane A / money and evidence

Durable ingress, then asynchronous projection

The source command appends canonical pending ingress in its transaction. Best-effort scheduling later creates versioned facts and bounded projections. A durable failure ledger and store-scoped recovery sweep retain unscheduled or failed work without rolling back the business operation.

Source transition -> pending ingress -> facts -> attributed projections -> recovery
Lane B / inventory

Typed effect inside the source transaction

The same command records movement evidence, SKU Activity, quantity, and valuation intent atomically. Before activation, compatibility shadow preserves legacy balance authority; after activation, the kernel owns authoritative quantity and valuation.

Source transition -> inventory effect -> balance, evidence, and valuation state

Compatibility shadow

All writers use the effect boundary, but legacy stock remains authoritative while typed effects are compared.

Verified candidate

Bounded rebuild, catch-up, and exact reconciliation prove a generation; verification alone does not serve it.

Active per store

An explicit compare-and-swap cutover selects the generation for reads and can later hand inventory authority to the kernel.

Before and After

BeforeOperational records without a shared reporting contract

  • Live and historical paths could identify the same business event differently.
  • Missing cost could collapse known and unknown COGS into one ambiguous number.
  • Calendar-day comparisons could ignore the store's operating schedule.
  • Recalculation risked depending on current operational state instead of immutable evidence.
  • Direct inventory writers made a complete movement ledger difficult to guarantee.

AfterVersioned facts with governed projection ownership

  • Canonical source identity plus a semantic fingerprint makes live, retry, and backfill overlap idempotent.
  • Partial known COGS and uncovered quantity stay visible; unknown is never zero.
  • Store Schedule defines operating periods and same-elapsed comparisons.
  • Bounded rebuilds create candidate projections and cut over without touching trade state.
  • Authoritative inventory effects cover all supported movement paths and retain evidence.

Failure Boundaries

Missing cost

The transaction succeeds. Reporting records partial coverage, withholds unsupported profit, and creates a correction route.

Duplicate or conflicting fact

An identical fingerprint is idempotent. A different fingerprint for the same canonical identity is quarantined and cannot be certified.

Scheduler outage

Durable pending ingress and a bounded recovery ledger remain available for retry. Source operations do not fail because reporting scheduling failed.

Late occurrence

A late effect is accepted without blocking trade. Bounded occurrence replay stages a candidate deficit ledger and either switches lot and financial authority atomically or withholds unsupported cost/profit while the position remains rebuild-required.

Oversell

The inventory projection records the deficit explicitly. It does not invent stock or reject the completed business event.

Wrong store or role

Public reads and corrections fail closed unless the actor is a full admin for the requested store.

How to Extend This Safely

  • Add a versioned fact or contribution contract before adding a metric to a view.
  • Preserve canonical identity and semantic fingerprints across live, retry, and backfill paths; do not invent a backfill-only key.
  • Capture recognition-time commercial attribution before resolving provisional SKU lineage into canonical SKU projections.
  • Keep revenue currency and inventory valuation currency explicit. Withhold derived profit when conversion evidence is absent.
  • Route missing inputs to the existing domain owner and keep the underlying business workflow available.
  • Build new summaries as replaceable projections with bounded reads, health state, evidence links, and reconciliation.
  • Before any production activation, run the normalized-email migration, bounded backfill, shadow comparison, reconciliation, and rollback rehearsal against production-scale data.

Quiz: Pass Required

Score at least 8 out of 10. Grading reveals the explanation for every answer.

1. What is authoritative in the new reporting mental model?
Operational domains remain authoritative for business workflows. Reporting facts are the durable evidence used to derive replaceable reporting views.
2. What happens when a sale includes an SKU with no unit cost?
Trade continuity is explicit. Unknown cost is not zero, and partial known cost remains useful without overstating profit certainty.
3. How do live ingestion and backfill avoid double-counting the same business event?
One identity policy spans live, retry, and backfill. Matching fingerprints are idempotent; mismatches quarantine instead of certifying conflicting truth.
4. Why is reporting scheduling best effort?
Durability comes before scheduling. Pending work can be retried, while sales and other source operations remain available.
5. When may Athena calculate profit across revenue and valuation amounts?
The foundation preserves revenue and valuation currency independently and refuses to manufacture cross-currency profit.
6. What authority does the inventory kernel have before a store is activated?
Calling one kernel does not itself transfer authority. Compatibility shadow keeps legacy stock authoritative until a verified, reconciled, explicitly approved cutover.
7. Which boundary defines week-to-date and same-elapsed comparisons?
Store Schedule is the shared operational calendar, so comparable periods reflect how the store actually trades.
8. What is the difference between a verified and an active projection generation?
Verification establishes readiness, not authority. A separate store-scoped compare-and-swap activation selects what readers may see.
9. A future labor-cost metric does not fit the merchandise COGS contract. What should happen first?
A separate versioned contribution preserves merchandise semantics and makes labor evidence, unknown coverage, replay, and future projection changes explicit.
10. What is the finish line for this delivery?
