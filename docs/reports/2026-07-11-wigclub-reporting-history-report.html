Executive summary

Problem

Most pre-schedule Wigclub revenue could not become reporting facts because period and currency evidence was absent. A prior preview found 1,726 candidates, only 3 eligible, 1,691 quarantined, and 32 payment conflicts.

Resolution

One authenticated full admin drafts a scoped historical interpretation policy and a different authenticated full admin approves it. A complete read-only preflight then seals exact candidate semantics into a reporting-owned manifest; apply consumes only that manifest.

Current boundary

The approved Mon–Sat 09:00–23:00, Sunday-closed policy produced 1,083 canonical facts. Fifty Sunday events are policy-excluded, not quarantined. Disposable prototype procurement records were removed from dev. Revenue and quantity coverage is complete; cost-limited metrics publish known components with explicit uncosted coverage.

Mental model

Admin A drafts store- and interval-scoped historical periods plus GHS revenue evidence.
A different full admin approves immutable content; server identities and an approval hash prove dual control.
Preview and preflight scan every bounded source lane and fail closed on conflicts or drift.
Exact parity seals an ordered manifest containing sanitized, resolved candidate semantics.
Apply reads manifest pages only, producing idempotent facts with immutable provenance and policy lineage.
Projections, reconciliation, and public reads preserve that lineage; only a verified generation may activate.

  • The compatibility implementation does not rewrite POS, payments, expenses, storefront, service, inventory, SKU, or Store Schedule rows. A separate one-time dev-only cleanup discarded disposable prototype procurement source/reporting rows.
  • Live ingress and ordinary schedule consumers do not read the compatibility policy.
  • Current-inventory authority and cutover remain unchanged.
  • Failed/cancelled manifests are cleanup-eligible after 7 days and completed manifests after 90 days; durable fact evidence remains.
  • Only projection selection supports rollback; accepted historical facts are incremental and idempotent, not transactionally undone.
