fix: Treat linked pending checkout items as trusted SKU aliases

Once a pending checkout item is linked to a trusted SKU, POS and operational read models should resolve it as that trusted SKU while preserving the pending item as server-owned, role-appropriate audit provenance.

Type: fix Status: active Date: 2026-07-03 Worktree: .worktrees/codex/pending-checkout-sku-alias-plan Source: Markdown plan

Direction

Resolve

Linked pending items become aliases to approved trusted product/SKU ids through one shared server-side resolver.

Preserve

Keep pendingCheckoutItemId, provisional anchors, cashier label, sold quantity, and local sync identity in server records, with redacted role-aware DTO exposure.

Avoid

No destructive historical row rewrite, no mutable report drift, no React-only alias semantics, and no fabricated retroactive stock movement.

Guardrails

  • For v1, linked alias decisions become terminal after transaction attribution; relink after attribution is deferred to audited correction/history work.
  • Current reads after link show the trusted SKU; closed as-of reports before aliasDecisionAt remain reproducible unless explicitly reclassified.
  • Post-link sales store trusted SKU anchors and use normal trusted inventory/hold effects; pre-link sales keep provisional anchors and resolve through alias history.
  • Stored sale anchors stay distinct from effective trusted read identity and stock mutation sources.
  • Linked lookup codes use a posPendingCheckoutLookupAlias projection with store/code and store/SKU status indexes when the trusted SKU already has a barcode.
  • Linked pending sale evidence does not change trusted availability without a real repair or correction command.
  • Public, storefront-facing, and low-privilege DTOs do not expose raw pending provenance or conflict details.

Primary Surfaces

  • POS catalog, barcode lookup, full snapshot, search, and cart projection show the trusted SKU without duplicate provisional rows.
  • Session, expense, checkout completion, and offline sync use the same alias classification.
  • Transaction detail, Daily Operations, product timelines, store pulse, and SKU activity attribute linked pending sale activity to the trusted SKU.
  • Voids and adjustments attribute corrections to the trusted SKU while reversing only inventory effects that actually exist.

Implementation Units

U1. Shared resolver

Add pendingCheckoutSkuResolution with linked, unresolved, finalized, and invalid outcomes plus terminal-after-attribution and bounded index/query contracts.

U2. POS catalog and lookup

Make snapshots, lookup codes, barcode search, availability, and pending visibility alias-aware through the lookup alias projection without overwriting trusted SKU barcodes.

U3. Session and cart commands

Use trusted identity for linked aliases in active register and expense flows while preserving pending provenance.

U4. Checkout and offline sync

Settle pre-link historical evidence through immutable alias attribution, and store trusted anchors for post-link sales with normal trusted inventory behavior.

U5. Voids, adjustments, and SKU activity

Attribute ledger activity to the trusted SKU without inventing historical stock movements.

U6. Read models and analytics

Update transaction detail, Daily Operations, product timelines, store pulse, and operational events to consume resolved sale-item identity and show authorized reconciliation state when linked history lacks stock repair.

U7. Frontend presentation parity

Simplify POS search/cart/ProductStock behavior around resolved DTOs and avoid React-side alias truth forks.

U8. Docs and verification

Document alias immutability, DTO exposure, lookup aliases, stock availability invariants, focused tests, and graph rebuild requirements.

Highest-Risk Checks

Inventory drift

Linked alias attribution must not create stock movement that did not happen through a source command.

Report drift

Historical reads must not change silently after relink, reject, repair, or EOD without immutable as-of evidence.

Read amplification

Catalog and analytics alias composition need indexed or batched lookup paths, with high-cardinality tests.

Split truth

All command and read surfaces should consume the shared resolver instead of copying pending status checks.
